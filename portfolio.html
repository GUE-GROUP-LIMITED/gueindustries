Our Portfolio

At Aliwe & Gyuve-Ado LTD, our portfolio represents a commitment to innovation, excellence, and global impact. Spanning multiple industries, our projects showcase the expertise, vision, and dedication that define our work. Here are some highlights:

Gyuve Industrial Zone

We are spearheading the development of a food processing and manufacturing hub in Benue State, Nigeria, to address post-harvest losses. This initiative supports agricultural sustainability, enhances food security, and empowers local communities with employment and economic opportunities.

GUE Cyber Labs

GUE Cyber Labs delivers cutting-edge IT security solutions tailored for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in Belgium. We specialize in safeguarding businesses against evolving cyber threats, ensuring compliance with international regulations, and fostering a secure, resilient digital landscape.

Real Estate Development: Gyuve Estate Project

We led the development of a multi-use real estate project, integrating residential, commercial, and recreational spaces. This initiative focuses on maximizing investment returns while delivering sustainable, long-term value for stakeholders.

Education and Training: Aliwe University

Aliwe University pioneers Open Supported Learning with a focus on Blended, Self-Paced, and Mastery-Based education. Our institution combines innovative digital resources with traditional methods, creating a dynamic environment where students thrive and achieve excellence at their own pace.

Our Global Reach

With operations across Africa, Europe, and beyond, Aliwe & Gyuve-Ado LTD is driving transformative projects that span continents. From cutting-edge cybersecurity in Belgium to sustainable agricultural development in Nigeria, our work reflects a deep commitment to innovation, sustainability, and progress.
