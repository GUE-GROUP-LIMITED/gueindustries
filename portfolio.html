Our Vision for Africa

As a new manufacturing company, GUE INDUSTRIES LIMITED is positioned to leverage the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) agreement to expand market access and drive intra-African trade in quality consumer goods.

  • AfCFTA Opportunities
  • Tariff-Free Access: Leveraging AfCFTA to eliminate trade barriers and expand into continental markets with zero to reduced tariffs.
  • Local Manufacturing: Starting production in Nigeria with plans to scale across West Africa and beyond under AfCFTA frameworks.
  • Pan-African Vision: Building a continental supply chain to serve 1.3 billion Africans with affordable, quality products made in Africa.
